Stranger Things is a television-format series created by The Duffer Brothers. It was first released as a Netflix original series on July 15, 2016. The show takes place in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana in the early-to-mid 1980s.

The first season revolves around the disappearance of Will Byers, while the second season explores the repercussions of the mysterious events of first season. The third season focuses on "forces of evil that are new."The upcoming fourth season will explore newer regions, outside of Hawkins, like California.
